    SCHOOL
    Robotics (The Robo King)

    Learn the basics of robotics and robot building. Learn about Arduino and how to interact with the software. Learn about the various hardware component and how to bring them to use. The Robotics module for the School going students is specifically designed to bring in more practicality to the teaching and is taught in a way that the students can easily learn it. The students Learn with a hand on experience.

    Website Designing (The Web King)

    Come be a part of the World of Internet by learning how the internet works and how to design websites for the Internet. Learn the basics of Web development like HTML , CSS and other technologies and create your own website ready to be published on the Internet

    The Basic Beginner ?

    We help the students in learning the basics of the Computer and its various Technologies. Get introduced to some of the more common technologies of IT industry. This course is customizable according to the needs. This includes C/C++, Java, Python and some other Technologies.

    COLLEGE
    Robotics – Amateur mode

    Learn the basics of robotics and get introduced to this wonderful world. You start off from the basics of electronics and then get introduced to Arduino. By the end of this course you will be able to design your own circuits and make machines that are capable of solving real world problems. We have a project based approach to our teaching and bring in more practical and problem solving approach.

    Robotics with IOT – Legendary mode

    Jump right off into the Advance Robotics department with more advanced projects. Learn about IOT and its basics. Learn about working with cloud. Learn about home automation and make projects that require you to work out your brain cells.
